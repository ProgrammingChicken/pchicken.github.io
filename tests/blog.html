I don't even know..?

Posted on 1/19/2016

This is a filler post that I need to make for this blog project. But honestly, I don't know why I like to program. Ever since I was born, I had a thing for technology. I learned how to play online mario games when I was three. Wasn't too bad at them if I say so myself. So, the first time I learned to program was with Scratch (or Python?) when I googled for a minecraft clone for my 3DS (was really interested at the time) and got this weird site called scratch.mit.edu, and apparently it used flash so I couldn't play it on my 3DS. A few months later, I was bored out of my mind and decided to find another minecraft clone, and I remembered that site. I went to it and found out that it was an awesome coding site for kids that was free and easy. I ended up creating an account and getting tons of followers and making lots of friends. Yeah, that's pretty much my story. Feels good to put it on techno-paper. XD
